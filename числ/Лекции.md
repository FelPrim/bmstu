$$T_{n+1}(x)=2xT_{n}(x)-T_{n-1}(x)$$

$T_{3}(x)=2x(2x^2-1)-x=4x^3-3x$

$$\begin{gather}
x \in [a,b] \\
x=\frac{a+b}{2}+\frac{b-a}{2}t, t \in[-1,1] \\
x_{i}=\frac{a+b}{2}+\frac{b-a}{2}t_{i}, i\in\{0, \ldots, n\} \\
x-x_{i}=\frac{b-a}{2}(t-t_{i}) \\
P_{n+1}(x)=\prod _{i=o}^n (x-x_{i})=\left( \frac{b-a}{2} \right)^{n+1}\prod _{i=o}^n (t-t_{i})
\end{gather}$$
